Tracking Eviction Trends for FY ‘24 in Oregon PRESENTATION BY MINA MOHEBBI & DAVID CAMPER

The Eviction Research Network collects, analyzes, & maps eviction data while helping other researchers map & analyze theirs. For our Capstone Project, we were tasked with assisting ERN with completing, interpreting & visualizing the extension of their 2024 Q1 report of evictions in the state of Oregon.
