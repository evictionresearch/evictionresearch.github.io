Links to organizations that provide help to households facing eviction

Page last updated

Note

This page lists organizations that provide services and financial assistance to households facing eviction. We include this information for states where we have done research, and organize resources by state and county/metropolitan region.

Also see this national list of rent assistance programs that covers areas outside of our study areas

If you know of a relevant organization that is not listed here and you are authorized to make the organization's services known to the public, please send us an email at evictions@berkeley.edu that includes your contact information and the name of the organization.

Please contact these groups directly for more information.
