Resources

State, local, non-profit and legal groups to assist those facing eviction

Note

This page is organized first by state then by county or metro region in which the services are located. The content of this page is not itself advice but rather a collection of known organizations that assist those facing eviction. The manner of assistance varies with each organization and the extent to which a group is able to provide advice or assistance is not listed here. Please contact these groups directly for more information.

Available states

Delaware, Illinois, Indiana, Minnesota, Missouri, Pennsylvania

If there is a group that is not listed here and you are an individual authorized to make such services known to the public, please provide contact information and name of the organization. We will add it to the page with the next update.
