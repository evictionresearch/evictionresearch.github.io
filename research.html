Abstract
The burden from evictions is not shared equally in Marion County: nearly two-thirds of majority-minority census tracts experience high levels of eviction filings. Black and Hispanic/Latinx minority communities experience significantly higher rates of both eviction filings and evictions. A lack of timely, accurate, and easily accessible data poses a challenge for research on housing instability in Marion County
